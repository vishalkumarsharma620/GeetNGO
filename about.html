    • About The NGO

    Being a prominent NGO in India, GEET Foundation is dedicated to empowering individuals and creating a brighter future through access to quality healthcare, education, and safe shelter. We work to provide comprehensive support to vulnerable communities by offering medical care, scholarship opportunities, and building shelters to ensure that no one is left without the basic necessities. Our initiatives focus on improving lives at the grassroots level while collaborating with the government to influence public policy and create systemic change. As one of the most trusted NGOs in India, our programs are designed to meet the needs of individuals in critical areas, helping them achieve better health, education, and a secure place to call home.
